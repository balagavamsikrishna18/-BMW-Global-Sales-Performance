# BMW Global Sales Performance  (Power BI Interactive Dashboard)

## Project Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze BMW’s global sales performance from **2010 to 2024** using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The dashboard helps business stakeholders understand revenue trends, fuel-type performance, regional contribution, engine-size distribution, and color-based customer preferences. These insights support strategic decision‑making and help forecast sales growth.

## Questions (KPIs Covered)
- What is the **total revenue**, **sales volume**, and **cars sold** from 2010–2024?
- Which **fuel type** generated the highest revenue?
- Which **region** contributed the most to BMW’s sales?
- Which **car color** performed the best in terms of revenue?
- How does **engine size** affect sales?
- How did sales trend **year-by-year**?
- What is the **quarter-wise** performance?
- How do **monthly** and **weekly** trends look?
- What is the contribution of each engine and fuel category to total revenue?
 ## Process
- Cleaned and validated the dataset for missing values and incorrect formatting.
- Extracted key date components: **Year, Month, Quarter, Week Name**.
- Created DAX measures for:
  - Total Revenue  
  - Sales Volume  
  - Total Cars Sold  
  - Average Price  
  - Fuel & Region Revenue  
- Developed visuals such as:
  - Bar charts  
  - Donut charts  
  - Line charts  
  - KPI cards  
  - Slicers for interactive filtering  
- Organized all visuals into a professionally structured Power BI dashboard.

## Project Insights
- **Fuel Type:** Petrol, Diesel, Hybrid, and Electric categories generated almost equal revenue, indicating balanced customer demand.
- **Regions:** Asia, Europe, and North America recorded the highest revenue, crossing **3.2T+** each.
- **Colors:** Red, Silver, and White were top performers, showing strong customer preferences.
- **Engine Size:** Revenue is well-distributed across engine segments, indicating diverse market interest.
- **Yearly Trend:** Strong performance observed during 2010–2015, with fluctuations seen in later years.
- **KPIs Summary:**
  - **19T** Total Revenue  
  - **253M** Sales Volume  
  - **50K** Cars Sold  
  - **75.03K** Average Price  

## Final Conclusion
BMW’s sales data from 2010–2024 shows consistent performance across fuel types, regions, and engine sizes. To boost future sales:
- Expand Electric Vehicle (EV) offerings to support sustainability trends.
- Focus more on high-performing regions such as **Asia, Europe, and North America**.
- Use color and engine-size insights to customize product marketing.
- Leverage month and quarter trends for promotions during high‑demand periods.

This dashboard provides strong analytical insights for strategic planning and improving future BMW market performance.
